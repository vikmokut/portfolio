Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

I offer software engineering services focusing on healthcare technology solutions, data analysis and visualization, AI/ML model development, and web application development. My expertise spans across healthcare information systems, electronic medical records, and predictive analytics.

How can we collaborate on a project?

Project collaboration starts with a conversation about your needs and goals. You can reach out using the contact form above, and we can schedule a call to discuss your project requirements, timeline, and budget. Based on our discussion, I'll provide a proposal outlining the scope, deliverables, and timeline.

What is your typical project process?

My project process typically involves: 1) Initial consultation and requirements gathering, 2) Research and planning, 3) Design and development iterations, 4) Testing and quality assurance, 5) Deployment and implementation, 6) Post-launch support and maintenance. Throughout this process, I maintain clear communication and regular updates.

Are you available for remote work?

Yes, I am fully equipped for remote work and have experience collaborating with clients globally. I utilize various communication and project management tools to ensure smooth collaboration regardless of geographic location.

Can you assist with existing projects or only new ones?

I can work on both new projects from scratch and assist with existing ones that need enhancement, debugging, or maintenance. For existing projects, I'll begin with a code review and system analysis to understand the current architecture before implementing improvements.
